Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I come here instead of an automated car wash?

You can find gentle car washes that won't damage your car but they're also not particularly effective. The more powerful spinning brushes come with a bit more risk as the dirt they pick up from other vehicles could scratch your vehicle.

How often should I get my car washed and waxed?

We recommended to have any vehicle washed and hand waxed at least once every three months, rain or shine. This will ensure the longevity of your paint and help keep the shine on your vehicle's exterior.

Will washing or waxing more frequently damage the vehicle's paint?

No, in fact by washing and waxing your vehicle you will help preserve the exterior by filling the pores and imperfections in your vehicle's paint.

Should I be washing the undercarriage of my car?

The undercarriage of a modern vehicle is going to be pretty hardy but you should wash underneath every so often. This is more important in the winter when you're driving on salty, slushy roads. That salt can be potentially corrosive and could damage your car. Rinsing this off after a drive can protect the underside of your car from rust.
