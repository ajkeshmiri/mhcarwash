Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my car washed and waxed?

We recommended to have any vehicle washed and hand waxed at least once every three months, rain or shine. This will ensure the longevity of your paint and help keep the shine on your vehicle's exterior.

Will washing or waxing more frequently damage the vehicle's paint?

No, in fact by washing and waxing your vehicle you will help preserve the exterior by filling the pores and imperfections in your vehicle's paint.
