Frequently Asked Questions

How often should get my car washed and waxed?

It is recommended to have any vehicle hand waxed every three month, rain or shine. This will ensure the longevity of your paint and help keep the shine on the vehicle's exterior.

Does washing the vehicle hurt the exterior paint?

No, in fact by washing your car at Morgan Hill carwash you will help preserve the clear coat by filling the pores in the paint job. By washing at Morgan Hill Car Wash the pores will be filled by a chemical base that is applied during the wash process to help maintain your vehicles shine.
